About 1313FOG

Our journey in fashion since 1998

Hello, customers

FOG is an Indian multi-brand chain store firm. We have multiple stores in multiple cities of Punjab. We deal in Men, Women and Kids ready-made garments like Jeans, Shirts, T-shirts, Trousers, Track-pants, Sweat-shirts, Hoodies, Jackets, accessories like Belts, Perfumes, Deodorants, Shades etc.

General manager, FOG, Bathinda, Punjab

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth story

1998

The Beginning

Established our first store in Bathinda, starting with a small collection of men's wear.

2003

Expansion

Expanded our inventory to include women's fashion and opened a second store in Barnala.

2008

Growth Phase

Added kids' fashion and accessories to our portfolio and opened our third store in Kotkapura.

2015

New Markets

Opened our fourth store in Zira, bringing our fashion collections to more customers across Punjab.

2023

Latest Addition

Launched our fifth store in Mansa and developed our online presence to connect better with customers.

2025

The Future

Continuing to grow our brand, enhance customer experience, and expand our fashion offerings.
