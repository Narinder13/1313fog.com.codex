Exchange Policy

Our policy regarding exchanges

1. Exchange Eligibility

We offer an exchange policy of 7 days from the date of purchase. To be eligible for a return, the item must be in the same condition in which you received it. It must be unused and in its original packaging.

2. Exchange Process

To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer service team at 1313fog@gmail.com or +91 98584 00013. You will be provided with instructions on how to exchange the item. You are responsible for the cost of shipping the item back to us.

3. Exchange

Upon receiving the returned item and inspecting its condition, we will process your exchange.

4. Non-Returnable Items

Some items are not eligible for return, including:

  • Items that have been used or damaged after receipt.
  • Personalized or custom-made items.
  • Accessories like Socks, Watches, Shades, Belts, etc. are non-returnable.
  • For hygiene reasons, items such as Underwear, Swimwear, and accessories are not eligible for exchange unless they are faulty.
